Caring for Your Cake - Tips for Serving and Storing Delicious Delights!

Pickup and carry the cake box from the bottom only.

Keep the cake level at all times including transport and refrigeration.

Transport the cake in an air-conditioned vehicle away from loose objects.

Store cake in the box in a refrigerator.

Keep away form direct sunlight, moisture and heat when not in fridge.

Take the cake out of the fridge 1 hour before serving and serve at room temperature.

Use a sturdy cake stand at least an inch larger than the cake board.

Store the leftover cake in the fridge in an airtight container.
